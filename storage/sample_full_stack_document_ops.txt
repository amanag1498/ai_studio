Summary

Priya Sharma is requesting approval to work remotely from Toronto for four months while supporting enterprise customers in the North America region. The employee will access CRM, support ticketing, customer policy documents, and internal knowledge base systems from a company-managed laptop.

Business Justification

The request supports continuity for three high-priority enterprise accounts during a temporary relocation. Priya is the primary customer success owner for two renewal conversations and one escalated implementation. Delaying approval may create customer response gaps during the renewal period.

All remote work must use multi-factor authentication. Customer data must stay on the company-managed device and may not be copied to personal cloud storage, USB drives, or personal email. The employee must use the company VPN for CRM, support ticketing, and knowledge base access. Public Wi-Fi is allowed only when routed through the VPN. The employee must not access customer data from shared computers, hotel business centers, or unmanaged mobile devices.

Approval is conditional on Security confirming device compliance before the start date. Customer Success leadership must confirm coverage for after-hours urgent tickets. The employee must complete a remote work security checklist and acknowledge data handling rules before remote access is extended.
